About Us

The Institute of Investment and Wealth Management-Africa is a Pan African Professional Institution established in Nigeria to provide investment & wealth management opportunities and promote activities of wealth creation and innovation management to members and non-members, through conferencing, executive programmes, continuous professional development, lectures, seminars and workshops.

Vision

To be the leading Professional Institution providing quality and reliable information on investment and wealth management including opportunities in Africa, setting the agenda for change, economic empowerment, unemployment and poverty alleviation in Africa continent.

Mission Statement

To be proactive in providing quality and reliable information on investment, wealth, accounting, taxation, finance, marketing and innovation management, to members and non mebers, through conferencing, executive programmes, continous professional development, lectures, seminars and workshops.

Why You Must Join IIWMA

Diverse employment opportunities both locally and internationally, relevant in various sectors of the economy Accounting, Taxation, Finance, Banking, Insurance, consultancy firms, Manufacturing companies, Engineering,Public sectororganisationand self employment.

Frequently Asked Questions and Benefits

Below are some of the questions you may have about the institute.

What is IIWMA?

It is a PAN AFRICAN PROFESSIONAL INSTITUTION IN INVESTMENT AND WEALTH MANAGEMENT .

Is IIWMA Approved?

Institute of Investment and Wealth Management - Africa is approved by Federal Ministry of Education No. FME/ESS/EIA/ANPB/XXIV/234 and CAC RC7035622.

Who can join IIWMA? Members from any discipline or profession?

Members from any discipline or profession?.

Is IIWNA acceptable for further studies ?

As Federal Ministry of Education approved the instrute, members can use it for MBA admission and it support admission for M.SC programmes .

Is IIWMA acceptable for jobs?

Yes is acceptable for jobs in the banking industry, insurance company, manufacturing company, public sector organization. It will increase your marks in jobs aptitude test. As multi-national companies have created a department c! investment and wealth management.

What is the benefits of investment and wealth management to other disciplines?

Accountants, Tax Practitioners, Economists, Bankers, Business Managers, Statisticians, Lawyers, Engineers, all disciplines need ideas of investment and wealth management to practice their professions and discharge their duties effectively. Particularly in the areas of financial analysis and modeling.

BBENEFITS OF IIWMA MEMBERS

Below are some of the benefits for joining the institute.

1. No professional examinations for direct entry members presently.

2. IWMA is Pan African Professional Institution needed at this present dispensation

3. IIWMA is approved by CAC RC No. 7035632 and approved by Federal Ministry of Education No. FME/ESS/EIA/ANPB/XXII/234

4. Members are allowed to use the Institute's designatory letter after their names (i.e Mr. OiuKayode Oke, B.Sc, FIIWM)

5. IWMA provides members opportunity to convert HND/Advanced diploma Certificates to B.Sc programmes by research in foreign universities and MBA/M.Sc degrees within a year..

6. IWMA certificate is acceptable for MBA/MBF programmes and other professional institutions registrations

7. lIWMA assist members for job opportunities in local and international firms.

8. Induction is done twice in a year June/July and November/December every year

9. After induction ceremony, there will be seminars, lectures and workshops (quarterly) in a year

10. IIWMA make provision for international Training and Development in USA, UK, Canada, Germany South, Africa Ghana, Indian, Hong Kong etc for acquisition of business ideas

11. Enhancement of professional development in investment/wealth/innovation through Training/Workshops/Seminars and Lectures

12. Opportunity to become International investor, entrepreneur, wealth manager, accounting and financial consultant through IIWMA programmes

13. Opportunity for financial assistance in IIWMA Multi-purpose Cooperative Society

14. Members are free to attend Nigeria and UK, Nigeria and Canada investment Summit, Nigeria and South Africa investment and wealth management forum etc.
